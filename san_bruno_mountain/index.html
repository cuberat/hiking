San Bruno Mountain


Park web page: https://parks.smcgov.org/san-bruno-mountain-state-county-park

Parking: 37.695178,-122.434124 (map)

Park hours: The Park opens at 8am. Closing time changes seasonally, and is posted.

The park's address (555 Guadalupe Canyon Parkway Brisbane, CA 94005) will get you to the first parking lot, but you'll want to drive through and park in the second parking lot, if it's not full.

Parking is $6. Pay at the kiosk on the way in. There may not be a ranger there, so you should bring exact change.

Hiking route: 8.6 miles, 3-4 hrs Elevation gain is about 900 ft

The trail is NOT dog friendly 😞
