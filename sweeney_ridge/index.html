Sweeney Ridge 🐕

This is an intermediate hike that starts as short gravel road, then quickly becomes a mix of single-track trails and fireroad, surrounded mostly by chaparral and grasses. In addition to the views of the Pacific and the Bay, this hike includes a visit to the Portola Monument, indicating the area where the Portola expedition discovered the San Francisco Bay. It also includes a view of the Farallon Islands on a clear day.


More detailed description: https://www.hikingproject.com/trail/7016219/sweeny-ridge-loop
Park web page: http://www.parksconservancy.org/visit/park-sites/sweeney-ridge.html

Parking: The trailhead is at the eastern end of Fassler Ave, Pacifica 37.599358,-122.479014 (map). Park on the street nearby.

The loop:
  • 5.5 miles, 2-3 hrs, elevation gain 944 ft

🐕 The trail is dog friendly, so feel free to bring your dog.
