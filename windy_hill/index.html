Street address: Windy Hill Open Space Parking555 Portola Rd, Portola Valley, CA 94028.

Warning, do NOT just enter Windy Hill Open Space Preserve into your navigation system. It will likely take you to Skyline, which is the wrong entrance. However, searching for "Windy Hill Open Space Parking" will likely take you to the right place.

My usual routes:
  • 8 miles, 3 hrs
  • 6 miles, 2.5 hrs
The hike to the peak is about 1300 ft of elevation gain in 3 miles.


🐕 The trail is dog friendly (leash required), so feel free to bring your dog.
