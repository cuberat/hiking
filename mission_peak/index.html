Mission Peak 🐕


Park web page: http://www.ebparks.org/parks/mission

Parking at Ohlone College (parking garage): 37.528097,-121.912835 (map)

Trailhead: 37.527314,-121.912648 (map)
Route: http://www.mapmyhike.com/workout/1123457471. It's about 7.5 miles and usually takes just under 3 hours. The elevation gain is around 2000 ft, so it's not a beginner hike.

🐕 The trail is dog friendly, so you're welcome to bring your furry friends.

I park at Ohlone college. If you take the Pine St entrance, you'll see the parking garage on the left. The fee is $4 for the day. The payment kiosk is across the garage from the first entrance.

The trailhead is on Pine St across from the parking garage.
