Mount Umunhum

Previous Next
Park web page: https://www.openspace.org/mount-umunhum-sierra-azul

Parking (be there before 8 am if you want parking):
  • The parking lot entrace is at 37.15951,-121.87577
  • In Waze, search for "Parking - Bald Mountain Trailhead", or click here to open Waze with the correct address
  • In Google Maps, search for "Sierra Azul Open Space Preserve Mt Umunhum Rd Parking Lot": https://goo.gl/maps/igPaDYXEkAk
  • In Apple Maps, search for "Bald Mountain Parking Area"
The trail head for the summit is across the street from the parking lot.

Route:
  • 8.7 miles, 3 hrs
The hike to the peak is 1227 ft of elevation gain in a little over 4 miles.

The trail is NOT dog friendly 😞.

More photos from Mount Umunhum on Flickr: https://www.flickr.com/photos/regexman/albums/72157688395689911
