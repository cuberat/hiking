Rancho San Antonio

Park web page: http://www.openspace.org/preserves/pr_rancho_san_antonio.asp
Parking at 37.332653,-122.087379 [directions]

Hours: open half an hour before sunrise to half an hour after sunset

My usual routes:
  • 7.5 miles, 2.5 hrs, 740 ft elevation gain
  • 10 miles, 3.5 hrs, 1500 ft elevation gain


The trail is NOT dog friendly 😞.
