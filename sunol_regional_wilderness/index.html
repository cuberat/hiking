Sunol Regional Wilderness 🐕



Park web page: http://www.ebparks.org/parks/sunol

Weather forecast
Starting from Geary Rd:

Parking at 37.515504,-121.830669 ({% include maps.html addr="37.515504,-121.830669" latlon="37.515504,-121.830669" %}). Parking fee: $5.

My usual routes:
  • 6.23 miles, 2-3 hrs
    Route: Start on asphalt down Geary Rd. Once inside the fence, it becomes Ohlone Rd and is packed gravel. Turn left at Little Yosemite up Cerro Este Rd (packed dirt and gravel). Follow Cerro Este Rd straight onto McCorkle Trail, left on Welch Creek Rd, left on Cave Rocks Rd, left on Hayfield Rd, right on Canyon View Trail, left to go across the bridge and back to the parking area.
    Elevation gain: 1300 ft.

  • 10 miles, 1930 ft elevation gain, 4-5 hrs


🐕 The trail is dog friendly, so feel free to bring your dog ($2 fee). Once inside the fence, dogs can be off-leash, provided they're well under control.

There will be cows roaming freely inside, along with the accompanying smells.

Cellular reception is very limited in this area.

The park opens at 8 am.
